Origin Story

H-Thai-Ml first opened in 1980. It all started with the traditional family recipe that Mr. Sharman wanted to share with the local community. That is when Mr. Sharman wanted to open his first location in Downtown Las Vegas. 40 years later, the traditional recipe and the business remains strong to this day.
